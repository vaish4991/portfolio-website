How It Works

Our app lets you manage school-related payments through a secure and easy-to-use platform.

  1. Parents load money into the student's wallet.
  2. Students use the wallet to pay for school activities, canteen, and more.
  3. Parents track transactions and set spending limits.

Key Features

Prepaid Wallet

Load money into your child's account for school expenses.

Spending Limits

Set daily or monthly spending limits for different categories like canteen or activities.

Parental Control

Parents get real-time notifications of transactions and have complete control.
